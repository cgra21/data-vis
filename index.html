Voting frequency vs Income and Education

There seems to be a positive relationship between income and education. As education increases, so does average income.
There are very few college educated people who make less than $40k and there are very few High school or less educated people who make more than $125k.
A majority of those making $125k or more are college educated, and a majority of those making $75k-125k have some college education or more. Whereas a majority of people making less than $40k are high school or less educated.

2020 is widely different from other years, due to the lockdowns from COVID-19, the amount of absentee voters increased dramatically, and we can still see effects from that even in 2021 where the lockdowns were lessened.
